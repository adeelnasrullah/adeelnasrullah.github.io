I am a Graduate Research Assistant at the Emerging Embedded Systems Laboratory, University of Massachusetts (UMass) Amherst working with Prof. Fatima M. Anwar.

I design secure, and light weight timing services for resource constrained embedded systems. My research leverages sensing, and cyber physical system design and revolves around the following questions:

  1. How can we design efficient timing services with low computational footprint?
  2. How do we protect timing services in cyber-physical systems against priviled adversaries such as OS and the hypervisor?
